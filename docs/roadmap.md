# 🗺️ Roadmap & Future Enhancements

The feature roadmap for SmartFit AI highlights future enhancements for B2C and B2B systems.

## B2C (Consumer Features)
- [ ] **Advanced Pose Corrections**: Expand real-time feedback with precise angles and voice-over corrective coaching.
- [ ] **Social Workout Hub**: Enable sharing custom workout programs and streak milestones.
- [ ] **Wearable Integration**: Sync heart-rate and calories from Apple HealthKit and Google Fit.

## B2B (Gym & Enterprise Features)
- [ ] **Facility Smart Scheduler**: Automated booking systems for gym classes and trainer slots.
- [ ] **AI Occupancy Forecasting**: Predict gym congestion based on historical check-in logs.
- [ ] **White-label Customization**: Brand dashboards with gym logos and customized theme styles.
